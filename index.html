Good to know

Before you order.

How much notice is needed?

A minimum of 24 hours’ notice is required for selected orders. Custom Cakes, rum balls, Wedding Cakes, treat boxes, and Treat Tables may require additional preparation time. Please contact us as early as possible to confirm availability.

Where is pickup?

Pickup is in the Gloucester area of Ottawa. The exact address is confirmed after the order is accepted.

Do you deliver?

Local Ottawa delivery is available. Delivery details and timing are confirmed before the deposit is finalized.

How do deposits work?

Deposits are paid by e-transfer. The remaining balance is paid before pickup or delivery.

What about allergens?

Items may be prepared in a kitchen that handles common allergens. Share allergy concerns before ordering so the request can be reviewed.
